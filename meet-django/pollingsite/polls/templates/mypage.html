About Me

Hello there!
My name is Shir, and I’m a 16 year old girl living in Jerusalem, Israel. I was born on September 24th, 1998, and was raised until the age of 3 in Israel.

I go to school in Leyada which is both a middle school and high school here in Jerusalem, where I study computer science, biology and french, in addition to all the regular classes we are required to take.
Through out my life I’ve lived in several countries, such as in the Philippines and later on in Brussels, Belgium. I got to experience different cultures and meet people who come from different backgrounds,
and I believe these experiences helped shape the person I am today.
Travelling has always been a massive passion of mine, as well as photography and recently baking aswell! I also enjoy art and singing.
I speak three languages - Hebrew, English and French, though my french is not fluent, yet (I’m working on it)!
